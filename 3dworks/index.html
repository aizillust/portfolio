3D Works

Blender, Mineways & Printing

Statement

Using my Minecraft sculpture of Gawr Gura, I converted the build into a 3D model and prepared it for printing. What originally existed as a digital structure within a game was transformed into a tangible object.

The process highlights how digital creations are not limited to screens. They can be extracted, reinterpreted, and materialized through different tools and workflows.

Although the original sculpture exists in a virtual space, the final print gives it a physical presence. This shift changes how the work is experienced, moving from something interactive and temporary to something fixed and real.

This piece reflects my interest in how digital mediums can extend beyond their intended use, and how virtual forms can take on new meaning when translated into the physical world.
