hello!

My name is John.

I specialize in illustration for games, animation, and VTubers.

I focus on storytelling through characters. My goal is to show personality, emotion, and narrative in each piece. I want my work to feel alive and clear to the viewer.

I grew up in the Philippines and Myanmar. These experiences shape how I see and create. I am also influenced by anime, music, and story-driven media.

I am currently pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Arts at San Jose State University. I am also expanding into 3D to explore new ways of combining illustration, animation, and interactive work.
