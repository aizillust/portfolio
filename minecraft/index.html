Minecraft

Survival & Servers

Statement

The prompt was to create a digital sculpture in Minecraft based on an icon, and I chose to build the VTuber Gawr Gura. She became one of the defining figures of the 2020 VTuber boom during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Although VTubing is a relatively new medium, its cultural impact has grown quickly across global communities, fandoms, and online spaces. It has also influenced me personally, shaping how I see digital identity and connection.

Gura’s recent graduation marks the end of an era, but her influence continues. Her presence helped define a moment in internet culture where virtual personas became widely accepted and celebrated.

This piece reflects that impact. By translating her into a Minecraft sculpture, I wanted to place a digital icon into another digital space, reinforcing how virtual identities can exist, evolve, and be remembered across platforms.
