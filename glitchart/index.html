Glitch Art

Data Moshing & Data Bending

B.1 Original Image by @ninomaeinanis (Hololive)

Statement

This series explores identity through distortion.

Using glitch techniques such as data bending and data moshing, I altered these images to reflect the instability between a persona and a real self. The work is based on the VTuber Pomu Rainpuff from Nijisanji, where the line between character and person is often unclear.

I wanted to push these images beyond illustration. Glitching them allowed me to break and reconstruct them in a way that feels more honest to that experience.

The distortions are intentional. They represent fragmentation and the tension between how someone is perceived and who they actually are.

This work is both a technical experiment and a way for me to explore how identity exists within digital spaces.
