Behind The Avatar

Interactive Visual Novel ft. Twine



Behind the Avatar is an interactive narrative about a lonely student who finds unexpected comfort in an online streamer during lockdown.

What begins as simple connection slowly unravels into a story about control, loneliness, and a character who may know more about the player than she should.

Hint: This story has a hidden secret ending. You can reach it if you resist at every possible opportunity. Game state carries over between visits, so replaying until you unlock it is okay too. That path is also where my mini statement on the piece lives. Have fun!
