Meme Mashup

Photoshop and Memes

Sources: Knowyourmeme Topics: This is Fine & California Wildfires

Statement

This piece uses meme culture to create commentary through remixing.

I combined existing meme formats to build a new narrative. The main framework comes from the “This is Fine” meme, which I used as a base to reinterpret a real-world issue.

The focus of this piece is the California wildfires in 2025. I placed the character inside a gas-guzzling pickup truck, surrounded by fire, to reflect the tension between awareness and inaction.

The humor is intentional. It mirrors how serious issues are often downplayed or normalized, even as they escalate. By using a familiar and widely recognized format, the message becomes more accessible while still carrying underlying criticism.

This work explores how memes can function not just as humor, but as a way to comment on current events through remixing and context.
