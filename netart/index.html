Statement

I created a narrative net art project that reflects my internal thoughts when it comes to pursuing art. It takes the form of a choose-your-own-adventure, where each path represents a different outcome based on the choices made.

The story is centered around a recurring question. If I stop now, would my life be better? Or is it the right choice to keep going? These thoughts come up often, especially when thinking about stability, success, and what it means to pursue something uncertain.

Each branch shows a different direction my life could take. Some paths lead away from art, while others continue forward. The structure reflects how small decisions can split into completely different futures.

The ending suggests a version where I am able to do what I love for a living, but it is presented more as a possibility than a certainty. It exists as a quiet outcome rather than a guaranteed result.

This project is a reflection of my internal struggle, and how navigating art as a career often feels like choosing between multiple unknown paths.

Story Loop & Page Breakdowns

This choose-your-own-adventure story about choices an artist faces was made through HTML + CSS and GitHub. The pages themselves were built from a CSS-driven template for each screen. Because of this, I could reuse the same base HTML for about 80% of the pages and mainly adjust the CSS for each background. The buttons were done in a similar way.
